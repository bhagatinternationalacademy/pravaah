About PRAVAAH

A Modern Platform for Skill Development

PRAVAAH (प्रवाह) meaning "flow" is a complete training institute management ecosystem. From the moment a participant applies, to the day they complete their program and get placed, PRAVAAH manages every step digitally.

Built for training institutes, coaching centers, and skill development organizations that want a professional, centralized system to manage their operations efficiently.

Digital admission and document management
Real-time attendance and progress tracking
Automated enrollment ID generation
Participant portal with secure login

Platform Features

Everything You Need, In One Place

PRAVAAH gives participants and admins a powerful, unified experience.

Smart Enrollment

Multi-step digital admission with document uploads and auto-generated Enrollment ID.

Attendance Tracker

Daily attendance with subject-wise breakdown and monthly percentage analytics.

Progress & Results

Module-wise assessment scores, grades, and overall course completion tracking.

Notifications

Targeted and broadcast announcements from institute admin to all participants.

Document Vault

Secure upload and storage of Aadhaar, certificates, marksheets, and photos.

Secure Portal

Fee Management

Online fee payment interface with payment history and receipt generation.

Certification

Auto-generated course completion certificates upon successful program finish.
